Tools

The stack behind the results.

What I reached for when the product needed it.

  • AWS · Lambda · Cognito · CloudFront
    BuyMySpot

    Microservices for users, spots, leases, payments, and chat — scaled marketplace traffic while cutting cloud waste during the B2B transition.

  • Stripe · Stripe Connect
    BuyMySpot · Borderland

    Seller payouts on parking leases; Borderland event-access (“visa”) checkout — payments as product surface, not afterthought.

  • Turso · libSQL · Vercel
    Sculpt Architect · Brain · Finance · Borderland

    Edge-friendly data for instructor SaaS, internal knowledge base, event finance ledger, and live immersive-games backends — one stack pattern across products.

  • Twilio · SendGrid · Resend
    Borderland · Call for Crew

    Segmented SMS, voicemail, and game-night email sequences (registered vs unregistered, 5-day / 1-day / 1-hour) kept players on the same clock.

  • React · Next.js · Socket.IO · Mapbox
    All products

    Player-facing landings, seller dashboards, in-room game sessions, and campus map discovery — UI that carries both narrative and operations.

  • Figma · Guerrilla print · Physical builds
    Borderland

    Venue posters, 1,000 invitation cards, and a parameterized airship build tool — product ownership that extends into the street and the shop floor.

  • Cursor · Claude · Analytics
    Across the board

    AI-assisted shipping for in-room games and ops tools; GA / Plausible / SEO loops for marketplace and HQ growth decisions.
